// Common Questions

NOT GAMBLING.
JUST COMPETING.

Q
Is FinalCall a gambling app?

No. FinalCall is a social wager tracker — the same category as keeping score on a napkin, but on your phone. There's no house, no odds engine, no sportsbook. It's friends making friendly bets, tracked officially.

Q
Does FinalCall ever touch my money?

Never. FinalCall does not process payments, hold funds, or integrate with any payment API. The "Settle Up" feature simply opens Venmo, Cash App, or Zelle on your device — like a bookmark. No transaction is initiated by FinalCall.

Q
Is it free? Does FinalCall take a cut?

Completely free to download and use. FinalCall takes zero rake, zero commission, zero fees. We're not in your wallet. We're just keeping score.

Q
How does the judge system work?

When you create a wager, both sides agree on a neutral third-party judge — a friend, mutual contact, or any trusted person. When the bet resolves, the judge reviews the evidence and makes the official verdict inside the app. Judges build a reputation and rating over time.

Important: FinalCall is not a gambling app, sportsbook, or payment processor. FinalCall does not accept deposits, hold funds, process payments, or facilitate real-money transfers of any kind. All wagers are informal social agreements between friends. Any monetary settlement is arranged privately between users and takes place entirely outside the app.
